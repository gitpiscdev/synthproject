home

TL is a an Artist who uses ausgewählte delicate Synth Sounds Repoirtoire from Balkan, Turbofolk and great unrecognised Compositions and performs them live through delicate Synth and Drum machine Sounds in a Live Setting. Aknowledging the TL mainly focuses on live performance while playing Synth Sounds through a guitar, a self build foot bass pedal and Drum machine. Mainly going back to a ausgelassen, günstige und spontane Art Live Music zu hören concerts a scheduled kurzfristig in öffentlichen Kaffees und Restaurants. Sound Beispiele can be listenend to on this Website. However, it is not the intention to produce regordings to listen digital, but rather create a concept which works in a live-setting but not so much in a digital space.
